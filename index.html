A Brief Background

Sneaky McGee is a third-person stealth game where the player sleuths about in the level, dodging cameras, deactivating laser gates, and stealthily taking out enemy guards, all in the search of the Absolt Drive.

Meet the Creator

  • Howdy Howdy!

    My name is Samuel Perry, and I am a graduating senior from CSUCI with majors in Computer Science and English. I've loved games for more than a decade, with stealth games especially being a favorite of mine, and so with the opportunity presented by this Capstone project, I decided to learn how to create a stealth game in Unreal Engine, Project Sneaky McGee being my first ever attempt. I hope you all enjoy it!
